The COMPASS Workshops are a series of weekly computing workshops designed to teach statistical programming and data analysis using R and RStudio. The workshops will be held on Tuesdays from 7:30 to 9:00pm in Room 307 at the McGraw Teaching Center in the Frist Campus Center at Princeton University. The workshops are open to all students, staff, and affiliates. To fully participate in the workshops participants should bring a laptop, although there will be a few laptops available at the workshops.
